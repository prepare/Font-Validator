Message: W1111

glyf: Duplicated knots

There are at least two control points placed at a single location on a contour. An off-curve point may have been moved over an on-curve point, or on-curve duplicate points have been placed by the font designer. In the example below, note how point 15 (open green circle) is behind point 16 (blue circle). The point numbers overlap.



Using an outline editing tool, one of the duplicate control points should be removed, being mindful of hints that may have been created using the duplicate point. Other control points may be renumbered and hinting information may be lost or altered.

For more information, see the glyf table specification.
