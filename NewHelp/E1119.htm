Message: E1119

glyf: Wrapped contour(s)

There is a contour in the indicated glyph that extends around and on top of itself (the on-curve control points are wrapping). In the illustration on the left, the contour appears as a square; on the right, with control points shifted, the wrapping of this contour is apparent.

 

Because the contour falls on top of itself, it may be hard to see in the outline editing tool. To fix the problem, remove the unnecessary control points.

For more information, see the glyf table specification.
