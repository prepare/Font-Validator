Message: W1112

glyf: Not all extremes are marked with the on-curve control points

The bounding box value computed from the control points differs from the value computed from the curves, indicating that extremes of the glyph shape are not marked with control points. On the glyph below, there are no on-curve points at the extremes.



The top and right side of the glyph below do not have on-curve points at the extremes.

 

For a composite glyph, the calculation is performed after transformation has been applied to each components of the glyph.

For more information, see the glyf table specification.
